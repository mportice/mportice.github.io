How to Design for 3D Printing

When approaching any design, it is important to consider the manner in which it will be manufactured. 3D Printing, though a very lenient method of manufacturing, has several properties that should be considered when designing parts that you intend to print.

Here I will go over some of the basic guidelines to follow as you're designing 3D Printed parts. Use these to consider the geometry and functions of what you are designing

A Quick Overview of how 3D Printers Work

3D printing works by laying down lines of plastic one layer at a time. This is essential to take into consideration in your designs, as 3D printing is a slight misnomer.

If we break down our thinking of the 3D printing process into how it builds up a model, it is essentially drawing out a 2D slice of your part, and stacking them all on top of one another. This is where the term slicing comes from.

This layer by layer process is the most important thing to remember as you're reading the rest this article.
